# VerdictCI Product Growth

Use this skill when the task is about turning VerdictCI into a product, not only writing code.

Read the relevant files before changing product or business docs:

7. `docs/scope/validation-plan.md`

## Decision Rules

- Optimize for a solo founder and fast feedback loops.
- Prefer proof from real AI teams over abstract market analysis.
- Keep the initial market narrow: teams already shipping AI features through GitHub pull requests.
- Do not sell enterprise governance before the CLI and Action demo are credible.
- Treat pricing as a hypothesis until repeated users ask for team/history/private-repo features.
- Avoid building SaaS before the open CLI creates pull from users.

## Useful Outputs

For strategy tasks, produce concrete artifacts:

- interview questions;
- outreach messages;
- landing-page copy;
- launch checklist;
- pricing experiment;
- onboarding flow;
- competitor-positioning table;
- founder weekly plan.

## Quality Bar

Every recommendation must state:

- target user;
- painful moment;
- promised outcome;
- why now;
- smallest validation action;
- signal that proves or disproves the idea.

Do not write vague startup advice. Tie decisions back to VerdictCI's CLI-first and GitHub Action-first wedge.
